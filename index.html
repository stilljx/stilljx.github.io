  • Start to blog

    2018-11-26 |
    • { lightening }

    Why am I trying to start to blog? I call it a love story. I might have fallen in love with someone or might not because it’s be so long that I don’t know how it feels. In fact, I am happy, stumbling, expecting, and upset because of someone I just met last week, and he just left the weekend.

    I think of the song “Cannonball”, so I sing to myself. Maybe he will know someday but I’ve decided to tell the connecting world how I feel not to keep my diary secretly until that I figure out if it’s love.
